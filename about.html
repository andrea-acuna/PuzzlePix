About PuzzlePix

What is PuzzlePix?

PuzzlePix is a picture-based word association game inspired by the mechanics of 4 Pics 1 Word, but with an engaging twist designed for elderly users. In each round, players are shown 4 related photos and must guess the common word that connects them. However, unlike the traditional version, PuzzlePix introduces a fun twist. Instead of immediately seeing all 4 photos, players reveal the images one by one by solving mini visual puzzles. After revealing enough images, they can guess the connecting word.

How to Play:

  • Observe the 4 images carefully.
  • Type the word that connects them in the input box.
  • Click "Check Answer" to see if you're right.
  • If you're stuck, click "Reveal Hint" or try "Give Up" to see the answer.

Project Info:

This game was created as part of a school project to demonstrate basic web development skills including HTML, CSS, and JavaScript.
